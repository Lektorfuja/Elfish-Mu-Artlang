Elfish mu is an artificial authoring language (artlang) for 
communicating in the forest with invisible elves. 
#######
First Edition (10 words)
(the words in brackets are in elfish mu)

1. Hello! (arimaku)
2. I (eg)
3. you (tuta)
4. My name is Elf (Eg name Elf)
5. Name (name)
6. Elf (Elf)
7. How? (Aka?)
8. What is your name? (Aka name du?)
9. You (du)
10. Goodbye! (blora)
